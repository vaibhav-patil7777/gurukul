About Our Institution

Excellence in education with strong values

Gurukul English Medium School & Junior College

Gurukul English Medium School & Junior College is a premier educational institution offering Nursery to 12th standard education, located in Mudi, Pr. Dangri, Taluka Amalner, District Jalgaon. Founded by Mr. Avinash Manohar Patil, the institution was established with a vision to provide holistic education that nurtures both academic excellence and overall personality development of students.

Our Mission

The school follows a student-centered approach where every child is encouraged to explore their potential to the fullest. The vision of Gurukul is to develop well-rounded individuals by emphasizing moral values, discipline, creativity, and leadership qualities.

Our Vision

To build a future-ready school system offering innovation, creativity and world-class learning with transparency and technology at its core.
